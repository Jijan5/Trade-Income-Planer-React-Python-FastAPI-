# Welcome to Trade Income Planner

Trade Income Planner is a comprehensive trading simulation and portfolio management platform that helps users plan their trading goals, simulate trading strategies, and analyze their trading performance.

## Features

- **Trading Simulation**: Test your trading strategies with historical data
- **Goal Planning**: Set and track your financial goals
- **Portfolio Management**: Manage your trading portfolios
- **Community**: Connect with other traders
- **Payment Integration**: Secure subscription management via Midtrans
